Background

This interactive demo visualizes how wearable solar harvesters track hand motion in 3D space. Select a trajectory, adjust the light source position, and observe how the harvested power changes. The simulation is based on a radiometric model that captures the geometric relationship between the hand and light source.

\[P = \kappa \cdot \mathcal{F}(a, H) + \epsilon\]

The harvested power \(P\) is a smooth, deterministic function of the cell's lateral displacement \(a\) and vertical separation \(H\) (‚àÄ \(H > 0\)), scaled by a constant \(\kappa\) that captures solar cell and light source properties. \(\epsilon\) accounts for noise.
